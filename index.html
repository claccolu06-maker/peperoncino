Our Philosophy

Artisan Pasta

Considered to be one of our main specialties and is strictly made on site using traditional techniques. Our pasta is made from the finest durum wheat, free range eggs and extruded using bronze dies producing a porous texture which absorbs the great flavours of the dish.

24h Fermented Pizza

Enjoy a lightweight, thin and crispy base thanks to the slow and natural rising over a period of 24 hours. We prepare it using exclusively Italian flour and fresh, seasonally selected ingredients for an unforgettable taste.
