Our Philosophy

Artisan Pasta

Considered to be one of our main specialties and is strictly made on site using traditional techniques. Our pasta is extruded using bronze dies producing a porous texture which absorbs the great flavours of the dish.

24h Fermented Pizza

Enjoy a lightweight, thin and crispy base thanks to the slow and natural rising over a period of 24 hours. We prepare it using only the finest Italian flour for an unforgettable taste.
